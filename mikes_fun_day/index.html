MIKE’S FUN DAY

Once upon a time, there was a man named Mike. Mike was a man of motion, always in action, and his life’s greatest accomplishment came on that fateful summer day when he created a spectacular volleyball pose that made him…

Sweden-famous.

Mike got so much positive attention for this brilliant acrobatic display that he decided to spend the rest of his life practicing and perfecting the glory of that one fine pose.

This is his story.

That day, he started by going to the city.

“Like Woah, Dude”

Mike was impressed with the breakdancers of Gothenburg!

He took dancing lessons and soon joined in!

Unfortunately, he could only do one dance move, because he could only strike one pose. Although it was pretty cool, the crowd got bored quickly. This prompted Mike to try another form of dancing… something he could impress his Hanna with.

It worked!! Hanna loved it!! But, as with most things in life, you can’t please everyone.

His friends convinced him to give up that goofy dancing at focus instead on his day job.

Mike said “Sure, dudes!” because he knew that today was Shadow Puppet day at the daycare he worked in!

“I’ve been practicing, this is gonna be awesome!”

His audience didn’t agree. It wasn’t awesome.

Mike wasn’t quite fired, but the higher-ups made him take extra evening classes to brush up on his teaching skills.

For some reason, this excited Mike just as much!

Why was he so excited? Class is supposed to be boring...

Well, as it turns out, he actually went to a surfing class!!

Radical, dude!!

“I’ve got it all!! I’ve got the sweet pose, I’ve got the sweet girl…”

“Now all I’ve gotta do is save the sweet world…”

and he lived happily ever after.
